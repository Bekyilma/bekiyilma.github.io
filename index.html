  • Me in a Nutshell

    I am a Computer Scientist with a background in optimization and applied Machine Learning. I received my PhD from the University of Lorraine (IAEM doctoral school) in Automatic, Signal and Image processing, Computer engineering. Previously I worked as a Research Scientist at the Luxembourg Institute of Science and Technology (LIST), Bruno Kessler Institute (FBK) and the University of Trento ( DISI).

    I'm AI enthusiast, passionate about applied Machine learning and Data-Driven Solutions. I specialize in the development and evaluation of machine learning algorithms, particularly Deep Learning and Reinforcement Learning for personalized and recommender systems.

    ✒ 🔎 Currently, I am part of the Computational Interaction (COIN) research group at the University of Luxembourg, Faculty of Science, Technology and Medicine (FSTM), Department of Computer Science (DCS), where I explore different aspects of Human-Centered Artificial Intelligence including Recommender Systems, Adaptive User Interfaces and Personalization in the context of Smart interactive environments. (a.k.a. Cyber-Physical-Social Systems (CPSS)).

    ✒ 🛠 I am also actively contributing to Brain Computer Interfaces (BCI) research in the framework of project BANANA, Brainsourcing for Affective Attention Estimation.

    I love nature, Hiking and motorsports. I try to put these together with my passion for photography. Check out some of my latest woks 📸 👉 Here
